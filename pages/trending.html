Trend Forecast

We anticipate continued growth in sustainable materials with an emphasis on transparency in production methods. Key emerging materials include:

  • Mycelium leather alternatives
  • Seaweed-based textiles
  • Recycled cotton blends
  • Biodegradable synthetics

Retailers who adopt these materials early are positioned to meet evolving consumer preferences and regulatory requirements.
