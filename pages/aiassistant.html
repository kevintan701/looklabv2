What are the top trends in sustainable fashion for this season?

Today, 9:32 AM

Based on current market data, here are the top sustainable fashion trends this season:

1. Recycled Materials

Brands are increasingly using recycled polyester, nylon, and cotton. These materials reduce waste while maintaining quality. Ocean plastic initiatives are particularly trending.

2. Low-Impact Dyes

Natural dyes and digital printing technologies that reduce water usage are gaining popularity, especially in casual wear and accessories.

3. Circular Fashion

Designs that allow for easy disassembly and recycling at the end of a product's life. Several designers now include take-back programs.

4. Transparency

Brands are offering transparency in sourcing and production through QR codes and blockchain verification.
