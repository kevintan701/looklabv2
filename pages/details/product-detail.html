Description

A minimalist approach to everyday elegance. This shift dress features clean lines and a relaxed silhouette crafted from sustainable linen blend fabric. Perfect for layering or wearing on its own.

Each dress is made to order in Emma Wilson's Los Angeles studio using zero-waste pattern cutting techniques, minimizing fabric waste and environmental impact.

This dress is part of Emma Wilson's commitment to environmentally conscious fashion. The fabric is woven by a family-owned mill in Portugal that uses renewable energy, and the garment is constructed in her Los Angeles studio under fair labor practices.
